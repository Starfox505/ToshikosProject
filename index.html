Welcome to Toshikos Japanese Cuisine!

Here at Toshikos Japanese Cuisine, we offer many different types of Teriyaki Bowls, Appetizers, Ramens and Udons, Cooked Sushi Rolls, Raw Sushi Rolls, Sashimi, Nigiri, and Handrolls. We ship many ingredients directly from Japan if possible, and our fish comes from San Francisco. We also offer Yakiniku tables in our Hanford Location, and while Reservations aren't required they are highly recommended. Our Dining Hours are 11:00am to 9:00PM We hope you dine with us soon, and we KNOW you'll enjoy the food!
